Recent Projects

ALS Assistance System

Bringing computers to all

Systems for those with ALS can be on the upwards of 30,000 US dollars. An amount most insurances will not cover, and most people cannot afford. The ALS Assistance System is a compilation of softeware, settings and custom programs to allow those with ALS to functionally use a computer again. With this system an eye tracker allows the user to click, type and use a computer as they would with their fingers. A custom keyboard allows for custom swipe commands and programmable buttons. Contact me for assistance with this if you need it.
